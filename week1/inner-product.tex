\title{An inner product space}

\begin{document}

\begin{abstract}
 The dot product provides a way to compute lengths and angles.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle


In order to do geometry in $\R^n$, we will want to be able to compute
the length of a vector, and the angle between two vectors.
Miraculously, a single operation will allow us to compute both
quantities.
